Background

PlayStation is a gaming brand that consists of four home video game consoles, as well as a media center, an online service, a line of controllers, two handhelds and a phone, as well as multiple magazines. It is created and owned by Sony Interactive Entertainment since December 3, 1994, with the launch of the original PlayStation in Japan.

Playstation has been dominating over its competition in terms of quality exclusives, which is one of the primary reasons to consider buying one console over another

Playstation exclusives this year include:

  • Spider-Man
  • God Of War
  • Tomb Raider

Playstation continues to deliver a entertaining and enjoyable experience as the years go on

For more information on Playstation, visit Playstation.com.
