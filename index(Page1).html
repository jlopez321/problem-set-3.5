Introduction

The modern world of console gaming has been steadily improving and evolving ever since it was concieved. As TechRadar puts it, console gaming back in the day was simple: " the decision was made by which games you wanted to play - Mario fans always went Nintendo and Sonic fans went Sega. Done!" A path paved by Nintendo and Atari with their early home consoles, console gaming nowadays is a fierce competition between three powerhouses in gaming;Sony with their Playstation 4, Microsoft with the Xbox One, and Nintendo with their hybrid console the Nintendo Switch. The following pages will delve deeper into each corner of the arena that is console gaming.
